Style Lounge Salon was founded with a vision to deliver a world-class salon experience, combining creativity, luxury, and innovation. The brand is led by Mr. Bittoo, Celebrity Hair Stylist and Founder of Style Lounge Salon, whose expertise and passion have shaped its identity in the beauty industry.

Over the years, Style Lounge Salon has established itself as a premium destination for Hair, Skin, Barber, and Makeup services, offering high-end treatments with top-quality products and skilled professionals. With a strong presence in Delhi, the salon is known for its modern approach, refined ambiance, and personalized services tailored to meet every client’s unique style and needs.
